We plan to launch a just-in-time (JIT) fresh produce delivery service operating from Mongu in Western Province to Lusaka. Our fleet of 5 refrigerated trucks will transport vegetables, fruits, and dairy products to supermarkets and restaurants in Lusaka.

Financial Projections:
- Year 1 revenue target: ZMW 4,500,000
- Diesel cost assumption: K20 per litre (fixed for budgeting)
- Net profit margin target: 8%
- Startup financing: ZMW 3,000,000 bank loan at 12% interest rate
- Fleet acquisition: 5 used refrigerated trucks at ZMW 400,000 each

Operational Plan:
- Daily deliveries from Mongu to Lusaka (approximately 600km one-way)
- Cold chain maintained throughout using truck-mounted refrigeration units
- Warehouse in Mongu for aggregation, no cold storage facility needed initially
- Staff: 10 drivers, 5 warehouse workers, 3 administrative staff

Tax Strategy:
- Register under Turnover Tax regime at 5% of gross revenue
- No VAT registration planned initially

Market Assumptions:
- Growing demand for fresh produce in Lusaka supermarkets
- Limited competition on the Mongu-Lusaka corridor
- Year-round supply availability from Western Province farmers
